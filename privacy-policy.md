  # Privacy Policy — SwipeDel                                                                          
  **Last updated: May 3, 2026**                                                                        
  ## Overview

  SwipeDel ("the app") is a photo management tool that helps you quickly sort and delete photos stored
  on your device. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

  ## Data Collection

  SwipeDel does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet
  connection and no analytics.

  ## Photo Access

  The app requests access to photos stored on your device solely to display them within the app and
  move selected photos to an in-app trash folder. All operations happen entirely on your device.

  ## Trash Folder

  Photos you swipe to delete are copied to a private folder on your device (`SwipeDelTrash`). They
  remain on your device until you choose to permanently delete them via the "Empty Trash" function. No
  data leaves your device at any point.

  ## Third-Party Services

  SwipeDel does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.
